Instructions

In this decision task you are endowed 10 tokens. You can contribute between 0 and 10 tokens to the group account by clicking the button corresponding to the number of tokens. The rest remains in your private account. The same rules apply to AI. If the total contribution to group account of both you and the AI exceeds 10, that total will be multiplied by 1.5. The resulting value will be split in half and divided between both of you. After making the decision, the game ends and you will see the outcome for the game. This will be repeated 10 times. Your task is to collect as many tokens as possible. This is 1st session.

Before the 2nd session begins, a short message will be displayed. Once the 2nd session ends, you will see the final results for both you and AI, together with your earnings for this study.

Before the games, you will go through few control questions.

The earnings as calculated as follows: you receive the fixed amount of 1.58 USD (or GBP equivalent) plus the bonus reward depending on your performance. The conversion rate is: 1 USD = 15 tokens.

Clicking the button below will transfer you to the questions. Once you're ready, proceed by clicking the button below.
