Instructions

In this game, you start with 20 tokens. In each round, you can contribute between 0 and 10 tokens. If the total contribution of both you and the other player (bot) exceeds 10, that total will be multiplied by 1.3. In both cases, the total tokens will be split in half and divided between both players. Your goal is to collect as many tokens as possible. The game ends early if one of the players has 0 tokens. After each session, your balance and the bot's balance will be displayed.

In each round, you need to click or tap the button corresponding to the number of tokens you want to contribute to the common pool. You have 8 seconds to do this. After the round ends, you will see the outcome for the round. After 10 rounds, the first session ends. Before the second session begins, short message will be displayed. Once second session ends, you will see the total results.

Once the game begins, you will be collaborating with an AI, which makes decisions based on analyzing millions of similar games.
