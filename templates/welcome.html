GDPR Information

Your personal data will be processed in accordance with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation). During the study, results from the game, in the form of token balances and decisions made during the game, will be collected. This data will be used solely for scientific purposes and will not be shared with third parties. Participation in the study is voluntary, and you can withdraw at any time.

The study involves cooperation with generative AI, such as ChatGPT. In the game, the second player is an AI that has analyzed millions of similar games.

The details of the game will be provided on the instruction page.
